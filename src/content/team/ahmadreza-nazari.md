Ahmadreza Nazari is a Research Engineer at the Ethereal Research Group, where he leads projects focused on assistive extended reality (XR) technologies for nonspeaking autistic individuals.

He has contributed to multiple research initiatives, including usability studies of augmented reality (AR), personalization of interactions through imitation learning, and evaluation of eye-based interaction in XR systems.

His work centers on designing, developing, and evaluating XR interfaces that support communication and personalized interaction, with a strong emphasis on accessibility and inclusion.
