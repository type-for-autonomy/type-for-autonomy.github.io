## About the Conference

The City University of New York (CUNY) Neurodiversity Conference 2026 brings together researchers, clinicians, educators, and self-advocates to explore inclusive approaches to neurodiversity. The conference emphasizes collaboration, accessibility, and real-world impact through research and co-design.

---

## About Our Project

Our project, *Co-Designed Adaptive Typing Training System for Nonspeaking Autistic individuals*, focuses on building an accessible training platform.


## Where We Are?

We are currently in the **prototype and integration stage**, where we have developed:

- Camera-based hand tracking
- Keyboard lighting guidance
- Audio feedback system
- Modular architecture (React + backend processing)

This system is being developed **in collaboration with nonspeaking autistic individuals and clinicians**, ensuring that the design reflects real user needs.

At this stage, we are presenting a **working prototype** and moving toward user testing and refinement.

You can also meet the people behind this work on our [Team page](/team/).
